About Me

I have worked for most of my life in the animal care industry. I spent 25 years working as a dog groomer. During that time I grew my own business, in 2017 i started teaching city and guilds level 2 and level 3 in Dog grooming at Duchy College.

In 2014 I was involved in a car accident which resulted in me being hospitalised and took time for me to recover. in 2023 I decided that due to my injury I could no longer continue grooming or teaching, both jobs I loved, but my health was more important. During the last 12 months I was determined to get back to work. Web development caught my eye, and simce then I have worked hard to acquire the neccesary skills to become a junior developer.
