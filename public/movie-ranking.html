Sarah's favourite Movies

A list of my favourite movies

1. Dances with Wolves

Maybe its the romantic in me but i just adore this Kevin Costner movie.
Set in the late 1800s around the time of the war of independence. It weaves the story of a young army leutenant who through his bravery wins a posting of his choice.
This takes him into the wild west and unbeknownst to him his future which take a very different turn

2. The Mother

This Jennifer Lopez movie is an action packed thriller with the right amount of sentimentality. Shows how beyond all set backs a mother will really fight to protect her child

3. How to train your Dragon 1 2 and 3

Some of my fondest memories I have are watching these films with my young son as they were his favourite too. How can anyone resist the cheeky charm of toothless and how the characters grow during the movies. Definitely a set of films I could watch over and over again.
